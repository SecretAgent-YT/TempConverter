To install, drag the TCONVERT file to your calculator's window in TI Connect™ CE.
DISCLAIMER: Program is subject to failing on the TI-84™, TI-84+™, TI-83+™, and the TI-84+ SE™.
This program will definitely not work on models including but not limited to:
-TI-83™
-TI-Nspire™
-TI-89™
This program has only been tested on the TI-84+ CE™
When your screen matches screen1.png in the Captures folder, hit enter to begin.
When you screen matches screen2.png in the captures folder, input a number 
between 1 and 6 to select your mode.
Now input your choice of measurement, and it will convert the 
temperature based on the mode you selected.
